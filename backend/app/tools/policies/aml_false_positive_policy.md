# False Positive Policy

Activity that is fully explained by the customer's known profile and a legitimate
economic purpose may be dispositioned as a false positive, with documented rationale.

Where the recipient is established and supporting documentation exists, the residual
risk is reduced. Closure as a false positive still requires analyst confirmation, and
the rationale must be recorded.
