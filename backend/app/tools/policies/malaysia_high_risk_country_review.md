# Higher-Risk Country Review

Business relationships and transactions involving persons or jurisdictions identified
as higher risk — including jurisdictions called for by the FATF — require enhanced
scrutiny.

The institution must obtain additional information on the purpose and the source of
funds, and obtain senior management approval to proceed where appropriate.

## System Action

The AI system must flag transfers to higher-risk jurisdictions for source-of-funds
verification and enhanced review.
