# Sanctions and Watchlist Screening

A reporting institution must screen customers and relevant counterparties against
applicable United Nations Security Council sanctions lists, domestic designated
lists, and politically exposed person (PEP) indicators.

Where a true match is identified, the institution must take the required freezing
action and report to the relevant authority without delay.

## System Action

The AI system must screen both the customer and the recipient, surface possible
matches with a similarity score for human verification, and must not clear a
potential match automatically.
