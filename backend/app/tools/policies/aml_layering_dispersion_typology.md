# Layering / Dispersion Typology

Layering disperses funds across many newly added recipients within a short time
window to break the audit trail and distance the funds from their source.

Indicators include a rapid fan-out of similar amounts to multiple new beneficiaries
shortly after funds are received. Such dispersion warrants enhanced scrutiny and
escalation.
