# Customer Due Diligence and Ongoing Due Diligence

A reporting institution must identify and verify the customer, understand the
purpose and intended nature of the business relationship, and identify the source
of funds where relevant.

## Ongoing Due Diligence

The institution must conduct ongoing due diligence, scrutinising transactions to
ensure they are consistent with its knowledge of the customer, their business and
risk profile, and, where necessary, the source of funds. A significant mismatch
between a customer's declared income or occupation and their transaction activity
requires enhanced due diligence (EDD).

## System Action

The AI system must compare declared income, occupation, and account profile against
observed transaction behaviour and flag inconsistencies for review.
