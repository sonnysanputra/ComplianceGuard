# Structuring (Smurfing) Typology

Structuring is the deliberate breaking up of a larger amount into multiple smaller
transactions kept just below an internal review or reporting trigger, often within a
short time window, to avoid scrutiny.

Indicators include repeated transfers just under a threshold, to the same or a newly
added recipient, that are inconsistent with the customer's normal transaction pattern.
Such patterns warrant escalation for suspicious transaction review.
