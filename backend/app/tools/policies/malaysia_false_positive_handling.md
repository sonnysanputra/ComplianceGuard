# False Positive Handling

An alert may be assessed as a false positive where the activity is consistent with
the institution's knowledge of the customer and a legitimate economic purpose is
evident — for example, an established recipient relationship supported by
documentation.

A false positive determination must be documented with the supporting rationale and
evidence. A case must not be closed where suspicion remains.

## System Action

The AI system may lower the risk where the recipient is known and supporting evidence
exists, but a human analyst must confirm closure.
