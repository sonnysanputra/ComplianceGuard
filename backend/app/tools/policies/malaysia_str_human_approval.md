# Suspicious Transaction Report — Human Approval

A Suspicious Transaction Report (STR) must be reviewed and approved by a human
compliance officer before submission to the competent authority.

The AI system must not automatically submit an STR. It may only prepare a draft,
present the supporting evidence, and recommend escalation. The final decision to
submit, edit, or close the case rests with the compliance officer, and that decision
must be recorded for audit.
