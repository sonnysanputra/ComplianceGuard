# Money Mule Typology

A money mule account receives a large inbound transfer and rapidly forwards the funds
to one or more newly added recipients, acting as a pass-through to obscure the origin
of the funds.

Indicators include a sudden large inbound credit followed by near-immediate outbound
transfers to new beneficiaries, inconsistent with the account holder's profile or
declared income. Such activity warrants immediate escalation.
